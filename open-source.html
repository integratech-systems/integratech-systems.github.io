Engineering philosophy

Why we don't chase trends

We use NiFi even though people say "just use Kafka"

Because NiFi gives you data provenance, back-pressure handling, visual debugging, and 300 built-in processors. For complex ETL with many heterogeneous sources, it's the right tool — and our NiFi production experience proves it.

We use Java 21 even though everyone says "go Rust"

Because the Spring ecosystem, JVM tooling, and Java talent pool are unmatched for enterprise integration. Virtual threads solved the concurrency problem. Rewriting in Rust helps no one's SLA.

We use OpenBao instead of commercial vaults

Full control, no vendor lock-in, no licensing fees at scale. We self-host and operate OpenBao in production for our own infrastructure and clients — we know its failure modes and operational patterns.

We build self-hosted infrastructure by default

Headscale, Prosody, OpenBao — not because cloud is bad, but because for financial companies and regulated industries, data sovereignty and operational independence are a business requirement, not a preference.
